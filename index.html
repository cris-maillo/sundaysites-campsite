not much of a camper

places I have camped in

My primary school organized a one-off camping stay at the school football fields for the eldest year group. It finally became our turn, and looking back, I remember staying in my best friend's massive camel-coloured camping tent and having mini variety pack cereal boxes for breakfast.
Two of my close school friends use to celebrate their birthday at their dad's house. He also had a basketball arcade machine. One year, we camped in their lawn. I remember the group of boys splitting a single can of beer around and being extremely angry at them for not letting me sleep.
My dad organized this hike up Peña Ubiña. Him, my brother, and I were joined by a few other family friends. I remember seeing a Moka pot for the first time there, not much else. Looking back, as I do quite often, I wish I had been more open to camping and going on hikes with him.
My brother and I stayed here for a week when I was 15 or 16 with my grandad. I don't really remember being remotely close to a beach. We stayed in his camping trailer that used to be stored in our garage. I remember the camping shop and having to paint the toenails of his now ex-wife.
